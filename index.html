WORLD SPACE WEEK - 2019

9th - 10th OCTOBER,2019

THEME : THE MOON - GATEWAY TO THE STARS

VENUE: LAKIREDDY BALI REDDY COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, Mylavaram

World Space Week (WSW) is an international celebration of Science and Technology, and their contribution to the betterment of the human condition. The United Nations General Assembly declared in 1999 that World Space Week will be held each year from october 4th - 10th.These dates commemorate two events, the October 4th,1957, Launch of the first human-made earth Satellite,Sputnik 1,thus opening the way for space exploration.Exactly 10 years later,on October 10th,1967 the signing of the treaty on peaceful uses of outer space was signed by UNO.Satish Dhawan Space Center (SDSC) a Statutory body of Indian Space Research Organization(ISRO) will be celebrating the "WORLD SPACE WEEK-2019" during 9th to 10th October 2019 at Mylavaram with the collaboration of the Lakireddy Bali Reddy College of Engineering,Mylavaram. Through the celebrations it is planned to propagate the benefits of Indian Space Program to the majority of Scientific,Academic and General Public and to spread the awareness about the Space Technology that will simulate the young minds and reap benefits for future generations.
